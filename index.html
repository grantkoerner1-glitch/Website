The Kitchen

Perfect Masala Chai

Learn how to make the ultimate warming cup using ingredients found in Aisle 4.

01
The Base

Bring 1 cup of water to a boil with crushed ginger and 2 cardamom pods.

02
The Tea

Add 2 tsp of Wagh Bakri loose leaf tea. Let it simmer for 2 minutes until dark.

03
The Finish

Add 1 cup of whole milk and sugar to taste. Bring to a boil twice then strain.
