Spice Knowledge

The Master Glossary

Turmeric (Haldi)

The golden soul of Indian cooking. Anti-inflammatory and earthy, we source our turmeric directly from Erode, India for maximum curcumin content.

Green Cardamom

The "Queen of Spices." Used in both savory biryanis and sweet chai. Our pods are hand-picked for their vibrant green color and intense aroma.

Cumin (Jeera)

Essential for tempering. Our cumin seeds are cleaned through a multi-stage process to ensure no dust—just pure, nutty flavor.

Kashmiri Saffron

The world's most expensive spice. We carry Grade A1 strands that provide deep crimson color and a floral fragrance to your desserts.

Helpful Information

Do you ship groceries nationwide? +
Yes! We ship via UPS to all 50 states. Simply email us at aroundtheworldmarket@gmail.com with your list, and we will send you an invoice.
Are there fresh vegetables available? +
We receive fresh shipments of exotic produce like Bitter Gourd, Okra, Tindora, and specialty chilies multiple times a week.
Is the loyalty program free to join? +
Absolutely. You can sign up at the register in any of our three locations. Earn points on every dollar spent.
Do you carry religious (Puja) items? +
Yes, we have a dedicated section for Puja supplies including agarbatti, lamps, murtis, and seasonal festival items.
