Our Story

Family Owned,
Family Grown

Around The World Market was built on a simple belief: everyone deserves access to the authentic flavors of home — no matter where home is. What began as the largest Indian grocery in North Carolina has grown into a true international market, welcoming shoppers from every background and culinary tradition.

Our staff treats every customer like family — you'll notice it the moment you walk in. The owner has been known to personally make sure a customer who couldn't handle the spice got some dessert before leaving.
